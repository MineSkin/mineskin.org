Hiatus Mod

Play Minecraft with your regular skin, while allowing MineSkin to use your account and change your skin when you're not playing!

The Hiatus Mod lets MineSkin know when you're online in Minecraft and automatically pauses your registered account until you stop playing.
And while you're offline, MineSkin can utilise your account to generate new skin data!

Source

The source code is available on GitHub.

Sign in with your Minecraft account to link the mod
To save your regular skin:
1) Disable your account on mineskin.org
2) Change your skin to your regular one (i.e. the one you'd like to have while playing)
3) Enable the account on mineskin.org again
